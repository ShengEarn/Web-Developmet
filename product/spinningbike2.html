Product Details

The Runtech IC6 Indoor Cycle uses the belt drive system. Unlike other spinning bike, belt drive system are very quiet and smooth when use. Belt driven spin bike requires very little maintenance as the belt itself is very durable and does not rust.


The 24KG flywheel allows the wheel to continue to rotate by inertia when cycle. Achieving a more fluid and consistent movement.


Saddle can be adjusted vertical and horizontally to adapt to most users height.


The adjustable resistance allows users of all fitness levels to benefit from the workout on the bike. The resistance knob is also equipped with an emergency break to stop the flywheel if needed.


The frame of the bike is made from aluminium & alloy while coated with a layer of coat to provide extra durability and scratch resistance.


The triathlon handlebar height can be adjusted vertically to adapt to different users. Providing maximum comfort when you train.
