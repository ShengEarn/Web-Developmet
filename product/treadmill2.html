Product Details

The FC PERFORMANCE RUNNER combines the quality and performance you desire with the flexibility of a machine that can be fold up and stored away. Design for home use, the treadmill incorporates numerous features that were developed with your performance and comfort in mind.


Running board is equipped with 6 level absorption system & air bag to reduce impact to the knees while providing the best running experience.


Includes Adjustable Cushioning System which allows user to modify the firmness of the running belt for a tailored experience. Adjusting the cushion on a scale of 1 to 4 (with 1 being the softest and 4 the hardest). With adjustable cushioning, the treadmill deck can be personalized to each user’s fitness goals while building up leg strength.


Monitor, record and customize your workout program by connecting to the Zwift or New Runway Apps via Bluetooth. You can now run virtually with other app users without leaving the house.


The LCD screen displays your time, speed, distance, heart rate, incline and calorie burned in real-time. Utilizing the information given to improve your workout for better results.


With over 20 levels of inclination for you to choose. Unlocking a wider workout variation and intensity for an endless challenging workout program.


The running deck gently lower itself to the floor when unfold, minimizing the risk of injury. Once folded, the treadmill can be move easily as it is built-in with a pair of transport wheels.


Equipped with powerful 4HP motor, the treadmill is able to provide you a steady running experience while also able to cater prolonged speeds of 0.8 – 20km/h with minimal noise.


With running area of 50(W) x 135(L) cm, providing you a safer and comfortable workout without limiting your speed.
