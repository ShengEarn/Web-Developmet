NOT SLIPPERY WHEN WET

Neoprene Dumbbells are known for its soft and smooth coating with matte finish. Despites having a smooth feature, it is not slippery and still able to provide grip for the lifter. Vinyl on the other hand can be slippery when wet; and may NOT be beneficial to sweaty palm trainers.
