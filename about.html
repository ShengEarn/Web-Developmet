About Us

The gym was founded in 1995 in Busan, South Korea and has
since expanded to have branches in Korea, Malaysia, China, USA, Japan and UK.
It offers a variety of equipment and activities including weight training, basketball, swimming, yoga and boxing. The gym has a membership system and provides private gym rooms and personal trainers who give professional training, nutrition and health advice. The gym also sells fitness equipment and nutrition food supplements. The gym has gained fame through students' participation in competitions and the gym's first multinational branch opening in the United States.
